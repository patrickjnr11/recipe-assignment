Beef Wellington

Beef Wellington is a classic British dish featuring a tender beef fillet coated with pâté and duxelles, wrapped in prosciutto and puff pastry, and baked until golden. It is considered one of the most impressive meals to prepare for special occasions.

Recipe Information

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Cooking Time: 1 hour

Servings: 6

Difficulty: Advanced

Ingredients

  • 1 center-cut beef tenderloin (about 2 lbs)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 8 oz mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 6-8 slices of prosciutto
  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 1 egg yolk, beaten (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. Season the beef with salt and pepper, then sear in olive oil until browned on all sides. Allow to cool.
  2. Cook the chopped mushrooms with butter until all moisture evaporates. This creates the duxelles.
  3. Brush the cooled beef with Dijon mustard.
  4. Lay prosciutto slices on cling film, spread mushroom mixture on top, then place the beef in the center. Roll tightly.
  5. Wrap the beef in puff pastry, sealing edges with egg wash.
  6. Brush pastry with egg wash, then chill for 15 minutes.
  7. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 40–45 minutes until golden brown. Rest before slicing.

Tips

Tip: Make sure the mushroom mixture is very dry to avoid soggy pastry.
